The Digital Romanistics community

Digital Romanistics is a bottom-up initiative that aims to address the needs of the Romance Studies community and to provide a space for dialogue and collaboration.

Everyone is welcome to contribute, for example by

  • sharing ideas
  • proposing topics
  • hosting events
  • providing feedback
  • inviting interested colleagues
  • translate contents
  • chiping in to cover expenses

One of our missions is to promote and give visibility to projects concerning Romance Studies that involve a Digital Humanities approach. In the page Resources you can see the list of initiatives we have collected so far. If you want to add your project to this list, please fill in this form.

The institutions that hosted Digital Romanistics events so far are

  • Glossaire des patois de la Suisse romande, Neuchâtel
  • University of Fribourg
  • University of Geneva
  • University of Lausanne
  • University of Zürich

Researchers from many institutions have participated to Digital Romanistics seminars, among which the Universities of Basel, Bern, Fribourg, Geneva, Lausanne, Zürich, the HES-SO (CH), the Politecnico of Milan (IT), the Institut de recherche et d’histoire des textes (FR), the Institut de France (FR), the Free University of Brussels (BE).
